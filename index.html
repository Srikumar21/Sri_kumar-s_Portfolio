About

I am Sri Kumar Reddy Valipireddy, a passionate and driven individual blending technical expertise with a deep interest in the wine and spirits industry. Currently pursuing my Master's in Wine and Spirits Management at Burgundy School of Business (BSB), I bring hands-on experience in viticulture, including Pinot Noir harvesting in France, and insights into winery operations. My academic and professional journey spans global consulting, sustainability-focused packaging solutions, and coursework in finance, E-Commerce, and global wine markets. I thrive in multicultural environments, value collaboration, and am committed to driving innovation and sustainability to make a meaningful impact in the wine and spirits sector.
